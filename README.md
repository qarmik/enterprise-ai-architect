## The Spark

This curriculum exists because of a LinkedIn post by **Mei Ling Leung**, Member of Technical Staff at [Embedded LLM, Singapore](https://embeddedllm.com/about-us/).

She described a CIO who asked: if Claude Code and Codex can now do most of what AI engineers do, why hire AI engineers?

After seeing what the *system around the AI* requires — permissions, tools, memory, workflows, evaluations, guardrails, monitoring, human approval, audit trails — the same CIO said: **we need more AI engineers.**

That observation is the thesis of this curriculum. The system around the AI is what this repository is being built to design, deploy, and govern.
